### Key Features

**1. Haptic Motor Feedback — Incoming Call & Device Find Alerts**
*Problem:* Silent notifications on a ring without a screen mean missed calls and lost devices. *Solution:* The built-in micro-haptic motor delivers vibration alerts for incoming calls and a bidirectional ring-find function — users locate their ring via the app, or press the ring to ring their phone. *Data:* Vibration intensity calibrated across 3 levels, <50ms response latency via BLE 5.4.

**2. Body Temperature + SpO2 + HRV — 4-Channel Health Monitoring**
*Problem:* Most smart rings offer only heart rate and sleep — insufficient for health-conscious consumers and medical-tier use cases. *Solution:* The R20 integrates a GOODiX GH3026 dual-PD heart rate sensor, CT7117 body temperature IC, and ST LSM6DSOETR3 6-axis sensor on a single PCB — capturing heart rate, blood oxygen, skin temperature, and heart rate variability in one device. *Data:* Temperature accuracy ±0.1°C; SpO2 range 70%–100% with ±2% deviation (lab-validated).

**3. Nordic nRF54L15 — Next-Gen BLE 5.4 with 12Mb NVM**
*Problem:* Legacy chipsets bottleneck app responsiveness, OTA updates, and multi-sensor data throughput. *Solution:* Nordic's 5th-generation nRF54L15 delivers 2× processing power and 2× energy efficiency vs. nRF52 series, with 256KB RAM and 12Mb NVM for firmware expansion. *Data:* BLE 5.4 supports 2 Mbps PHY for faster OTA, connection range up to 10m in open space, 5–7 days active use on a 14mAh battery.

**4. Titanium Alloy Body — 3.5g, 5ATM, Hypoallergenic**
*Problem:* Stainless steel rings feel heavy, trigger nickel allergies, and corrode with sweat over time. *Solution:* Grade 5 titanium alloy construction reduces weight to 3.5g (size 7) while delivering 5ATM waterproofing (50m equivalent) and full hypoallergenic certification. *Data:* 40% lighter than equivalent stainless steel rings; passed 48-hour salt spray corrosion test.

**5. Wireless Charging Dock — No Exposed Pins, No Oxidation**
*Problem:* Magnetic pin charging corrodes over time, causing charging failures and returns. *Solution:* Fully sealed wireless charging dock — no exposed metal contacts. Place the ring on the dock, charges in ~1.5 hours. *Data:* IP68-equivalent sealed design; 300+ charge cycles with <5% capacity degradation.
